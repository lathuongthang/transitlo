ABOUT

TransitLo is a multidisciplinary practice focused on architecture, exhibition design, and spatial strategy across China, Vietnam, and ASEAN regions.

We explore the relationship between space, behavior, and cultural context, aiming to create clear, functional, and narrative-driven environments.

TransitLo 是一个跨领域设计实践，专注建筑、展览与空间策略设计， 连接中国、越南及东盟地区。
